**What's Missing (for the agent to catch):**
1. No brand identity — feels anonymous/untrustworthy
2. Title is SEO-broken — no benefit, reversed word order, no model name
3. Description is poorly written — grammar issues, no formatting, doesn't address buyer concerns
4. No bullet-point features or specs section
5. Only 1 low-quality image — no lifestyle, no scale, no in-use shots
6. Battery specs hidden in Q&A instead of on the page
7. No capacity listed (how many oz?)
8. Shipping is vague — "5-10 days" from where?
9. Return policy is basically "good luck"
10. No trust signals at all — no certifications, no satisfaction guarantee
11. "Buy Now" with no "Add to Cart" — forces commitment, reduces conversion
12. No comparison to competitors or value proposition
13. Reviews are thin — no way to filter or see photos
